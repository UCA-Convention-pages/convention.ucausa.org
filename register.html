English Steps

  1. Click the "Register" button above to access our official Zeffy registration portal.
  2. Click "Regular Registration" and complete the required registration information.
  3. Click "Continue" to proceed to the next page.
    In the "Discount Code" field, enter the code:
    Enter promo code: XXXXXX
  4. Click "Check" to validate the code.
    Once the code is successfully applied, the registration fee will be waived. Please click "Checkout" to complete your registration.
